# Feedback Summary

## Feedback Received
- Make the key clue clear and known from the beginning or during gameplay, especially during interrogation.
- There should be more questions.
- It's very nice, I like it.
- Add new characters and improve the quality of the UI.
- We want to be able to ask your own questions.
- Not be allowed to ask all the questions.
- Make it more challenging by adding a timer variant.
- Add a map, make the board in the back interactive, or hide clues in the background.

## Which Aspects Of The Project The Feedback Addressed
The feedback mainly addressed gameplay, UI, and LLM integration.

### Gameplay
- Change the way questions are handled.
- Increase the difficulty by adding a timer.

### UI
- Improve the clarity of the UI.
- Add more characters.
- Make the background more interactive.

### LLM Integration
- Feedback indirectly addressed how generated content and questioning should work in practice during gameplay.

## Recurring Themes Raised By Multiple Attendees
- Increase the difficulty by adding a timer.
- Make the murder board in the background interactive or hide clues in it.
- Let players ask their own questions or, alternatively, have a limited amount of questions.

## Initial Reactions While Receiving Feedback
The reaction was a mix of agreement and surprise.

- Most of the suggestions felt fair and useful.
- The feedback gave solid insight into how the game could be improved.
- It was surprising how much the attendees enjoyed the game.
- It was also surprising how insightful and open they were when giving suggestions.
